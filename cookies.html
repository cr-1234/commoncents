Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how CommonCents uses cookies and local browser storage technologies to enhance your online user experience.

1. What Are Cookies & Local Storage?

Cookies and local storage are small text files downloaded to your device when you load websites. They allow our static site to remember details from your last visit locally in your browser.

2. How We Use Them

We use browser storage to provide functional consistency:
• Giveaway Tracking: Remembers your email so you don't enter multiple times in the same week.
• Custom Submissions: Retains any deal cards you generated via our Quick Poster, so they stay listed on your feed.
• CORS & Routing: Remembers sorted and active categories.

3. Affiliate Tracking Cookies

Third-party partner stores (like Amazon) use standard tracking cookies to record referral sales from CommonCents links, so we earn advertising commissions.

4. Managing Cookies

You can clear cookies and local storage at any time via your browser settings. Restricting them may clear your local deal listings or require re-entering active weekly drawing entries.
